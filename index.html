The Rittenhouse is to Philadelphia what the Plaza Hotel is to New York City. Like the Midtown institution, the Center City hotel has an illustrious history, attracts a monied crowd.

Experience the invigorating charm and rich history of Rittenhouse Square, one of Philadelphia’s most beloved destinations.
